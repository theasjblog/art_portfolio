If you are old enough (at least old as me that is), you might remember the 3D anaglyph effect. This was the prototype of much sophisticated 3D effects that came years later. This one is based, in simple terms, on shifting the red and the blue channels a bit (read a more in depth description <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anaglyph_3D" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>).

To enjoy this gallery you will need a pair of those glasses with the two colour lenses.
