I always find it challenging to take decent pictures of in cities. First of all, there are just way too many humans. I don't like to take pictures of humans, except for my own kids. Then usually I am out and about in cities during daytime, when the light is way too harsh.

The silver lining is that the combination of the over-population and harsh light really forces you to look for a composition. Geometrical shapes, light and shadow contrasts, black and white. You cannot just rely on Nature built-in wow factor.
